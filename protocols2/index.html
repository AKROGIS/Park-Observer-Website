Protocols

A protocol file defines the features you will survey and their attributes. It also defines additional characteristics of the survey such as how locations are collected. It is a text file that can be read by clicking on one of the links below. See the protocol specifications to learn how to read a protocol file.

How you load a file depends on if your are browsing this page on your mobile device, or on a laptop/desktop computer.

PC: Right click on the protocol you want, and save it to your your computer. Then install it on your device.

Device: Tap on the protocol you want. After it loads, tap on the share button, and select Open With.. Park Observer. This will add the file to Park Observer and launch Park Observer.
