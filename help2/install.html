Offline Installation Instructions

Installing Park Observer

  1. Download the current application package to your computer.
  2. Install iTunes
    • Coming soon...
  3. Connect your iPad to your computer with a USB cable. The iPad can only be synced with one computer at a time, so always try to use the same computer.
  4. Open the iTunes application on your computer.
    • NOTE: Version 12.7 of iTunes released in September 2017 removed required functionality. If you have version 12.7.x of iTunes, you must downgrade with a special version of iTunes (which can be obtained here) until this problem is fixed. If you see a warning about not being able to read the iTunes library, contact us. If you are only using iTunes to manage Park Observer, then simply remove the c:\users\{username}\Music\iTunes folder and try again.
    • NOTE2: Some users have reported that they can update Park Observer manually with iTunes 12.7.x by copying the update file (ParkObserver_1.2.1.ipa) and pasting it into the connected iPad (just right-click the iPad, listed under devices in the left-hand column in iTunes, and select paste). Dragging and Dropping the ipa file on the the device section in the left-hand column in iTunes may also work.
  5. Click "Allow" in iTunes when prompted to allow access to the connected iPad.
  6. Click the "Trust" alert on your iPad.
  7. Select the iPad icon at the top left of the iTunes window to see the details of your iPad.
  8. Click on Apps in the list on the left of the iTunes window.
  9. In the menu select File -> Add File to Library... (or press Control-O).
  10. Browse to and open the file downloaded in the first step.
  11. Click the Install button next to Park Observer in the list of Apps.
  12. Click on the Apply button at the bottom left of the iTunes window.
  13. Installation progress will be reported in the banner at the top of the iTunes window.
  14. When done, click the eject button next to the name of your iPad.
  15. Tap the Park Observer App icon on the iPad.
  16. An "Untrusted Enterprise Developer" alert will appear, tap Cancel.
  17. Tap on the Settings App icon and navigate to General -> Profiles&Device Management -> National Park Service and tap on Trust National Park Service. NOTE: You will need internet access to Apple's servers to verify the security certificate. It has been my experience that we cannot verify over the NPS network. If you have trouble verifying, either turn off WiFi and use the celluar network, or use a home or public WiFi connection.
  18. Press the Home button.
  19. Tap the Park Observer App icon on the iPad.
  20. Tap Allow when asked if Park Observer can access your location.

Installing Base Maps

  1. Download an existing basemap to your computer, or build your own with ArcMap (instructions).
  2. Follow steps 2-7 from Installing Park Observer
  3. Scroll down to the File Sharing section at the bottom of the page.
  4. Click on Park Observer in the File Sharing list. The panel to the right shows the Park Observer files.
  5. Drag and Drop the maps from step one to the list of Park Observer files, or use the Add button to browse to you maps.
  6. Some maps are quite large and may take a few moments to copy onto the iPad, monitor the progress at the top of the iTunes window.
  7. When done, click the eject button next to the name of your iPad.
  8. Tap the Park Observer App icon on the iPad.
  9. Tap the Maps button at the lower left of the Park Observer screen.
  10. If your new map does not appear, refresh the map list by pulling down on the list (press on the list and drag your finger down before releasing.)
  11. To use a basemap, tap on the map in the list.

Installing Protocol Files

  1. Download an existing protocol to your computer, or build your own with a text editor (instructions).
  2. Follow steps 2-8 from installing basemaps
  3. Tap the Survey button at the bottom middle of the Park Observer screen. (The survey button may be labeled 'Select Survey' or with the name of the current survey.)
  4. A protocol is used to create a new survey, so tap the '+' icon at the top left of the survey list to see the list of protocols.
  5. If your new protocol does not appear, refresh the protocol list by pulling down on the list (press on the list and drag your finger down before releasing.)
  6. To use a protocol, tap on the protocol in the list, and a new survey will be created from the selected protocol.
